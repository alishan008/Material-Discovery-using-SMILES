Enter at least 1 SMILES structure of a substance.



The simplified molecular-input line-entry system (SMILES) is a specification in the form of a line notation for describing the structure of chemical species using short ASCII strings. SMILES strings can be imported by most molecule editors for conversion back into two-dimensional drawings or three-dimensional models of the molecules.
Multiple polymers can be entered in the text box above with each line containing one and the model will adjudicate each as suitable candidates for further experimental investigation.
